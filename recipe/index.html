Stir Fry Ramen

Food Information

Why I choose this food.


I chose this recipe because it is very easy to make, and it tastes great. Ramen is very easy to get, and the recipe ingredients are very common. This makes creating this tasty dish very easy. I also chose this recipe because I'm addicted to ramen.



Allergy Information


Sesame seeds were are used in the recipe. Please refrain from eating if you have sesame seed allergies. Noodles are gluten free.








Ingredients



Vegetables

2 green onions
4 garlic cloves
1 cup green vegatables

Other

2 packs of ramen noodles
2 tbsp sesame seeds
1 tbsp vegatable oil

Sauce

2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp sesame/olive oil
3 tbsp sweet chili sauce



Recipe






Step One


Heat the vegetable oil in a pot or pan on medium-high heat, then add the green onions, garlic and vegetables. Cook for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender.





Step Two


While the vegetables are cooking, mix the sauce ingredients together in a small bowl.





Step Three


Boil the ramen noodles just until they begin to come apart and are slightly soft, then drain and add the noodles and sauce to the pot, mixing everything together on medium heat for 2-3 minutes.





Step Four


Serve topped with some sesame seeds and sliced green onions.
